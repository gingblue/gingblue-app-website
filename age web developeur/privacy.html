ENGLISH VERSION

1. Introduction

This privacy policy explains how Daily Core collects, uses, stores and protects your personal information. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices.

2. Information We Collect

Registration and profile data:

  • Apple ID account (secure authentication)
  • Optional personal information (age, height, weight, goals)
  • Language and theme preferences

Health and wellness data:

  • Conversation history with Mistral AI
  • Nutritional data and logged meals
  • Personal notes, goals and habits
  • Workout sessions and exercises
  • Progress data and statistics
  • Organization and productivity information
  • Financial and knowledge data

Technical data:

  • Device information and iOS version
  • IP address and connection data
  • Usage and performance logs

3. Data Processing Technologies

Firebase (Google Cloud):

  • Secure storage of all your personal data
  • User authentication and account management
  • Real-time synchronization between devices
  • Data location: European data centers (GDPR compliant)

Mistral Artificial Intelligence:

  • Conversation processing to generate responses
  • Nutritional analysis of foods
  • Personalized recommendations
  • Data processed in the EU (GDPR compliant)
  • Automatic parsing of requests into actions (tasks, goals, etc.)

Whisper (OpenAI):

  • 100% local voice recognition on your device
  • No audio data transmission to our servers
  • Real-time and private voice-to-text conversion

Shutterstock Assets:

  • Commercially licensed emoji animations
  • No data collection related to visual assets

3.1 Artificial Intelligence Limitations

The Daily Core AI assistant provides suggestions and recommendations based on machine learning models. Please note that:

  • AI responses do not constitute professional advice
  • AI may make errors and does not replace human expertise
  • Automatic parsing may not be 100% accurate
  • You can modify or delete any automatically created content
  • We do not guarantee the accuracy of AI suggestions
  • AI does not replace advice from qualified professionals

4. Use of Your Data

We use your data to:

  • Provide and improve our services
  • Personalize your user experience
  • Generate relevant AI responses
  • Calculate your nutritional data
  • Synchronize your data between devices
  • Ensure your account security
  • Contact you about important updates

4.1 Medical Disclaimer and Limited Liability

IMPORTANT - The Daily Core application does not constitute professional medical advice. All information, recommendations and suggestions provided by the application are for informational purposes only.

  • Always consult a healthcare professional before starting an exercise program
  • Nutritional information is provided for reference only and does not replace nutritionist advice
  • Daily Core is not responsible for health outcomes, injuries or medical issues
  • You use the application at your own risk
  • We do not guarantee the accuracy of exercise or nutritional information
  • In case of health problems, immediately consult a qualified professional

5. Data Storage and Security

Your personal data is securely stored on professional cloud servers with the following protection measures:

  • Encryption of data in transit and at rest
  • Mandatory secure authentication
  • Controlled and limited data access
  • Automatic backup and redundancy
  • Continuous security monitoring

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information against unauthorized access, modification, disclosure or destruction.

6. Data Sharing

We never sell your personal data.

We share data only with:

  • Firebase/Google: Secure data hosting
  • Mistral AI: AI query processing (anonymized)
  • Apple: App Store billing data
  • Legal authorities: If required by law

Anonymized data for:

  • AI model improvements
  • Global usage analytics (without identification)
  • Aggregated nutritional research

7. Your Rights (GDPR)

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Rectify incorrect information
  • Delete your account and all data
  • Transfer your data (portability)
  • Limit processing of certain data
  • Object to processing for legitimate reasons
  • Withdraw consent at any time

To exercise these rights: emoji.aichat@gmail.com

8. Data Retention

  • Active data: While your account is active
  • Backup data: 30 days after account deletion
  • Technical logs: 12 months maximum
  • Anonymized analytics data: Indefinitely
  • Automatic deletion after 3 years of inactivity.

9. International Transfers

Your data may be transferred to:

  • Firebase servers in Europe (GDPR compliant)
  • Mistral AI servers in France
  • Apple servers (App Store) with adequate protection
  • Always with appropriate security guarantees

10. Cookies and Similar Technologies

The application uses:

  • Device identifiers for authentication
  • Local preferences (language, theme)
  • Local cache for performance
  • Firebase Analytics (anonymized)
  • No traditional web cookies are used.

11. Changes to This Policy

We will notify you of significant changes through:

  • In-app notification
  • Email (if provided)
  • Updating the date of this policy
  • Continued use = acceptance of changes.

12. Contact

For any questions regarding this privacy policy:

Email: emoji.aichat@gmail.com
